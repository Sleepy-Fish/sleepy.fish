# The Induction of Sleepy.Fish

After spending years as founder Riley Harrington's testbed for half-baked and smashed-out-in-mere-hours
projects, sleepy.fish begins its life as a Real Gaming Company. With Lauren Grillz, Jeremy Doss,
Hannah Rucker, and Caleb Rogers joining him on a journey of gamemaking, sleepy.fish has awoken!

To start, our team is investigating a good first-pass of a native game, as well as developing a framework
for web games. Riley and Stu are exploring exciting and interesting things in light and raytracing,
Jeremy and Lauren are upgrading their C# and Unity skills, and Caleb is doing terrible things to
Javascript.
